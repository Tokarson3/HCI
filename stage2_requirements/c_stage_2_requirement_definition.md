## Scenario 1: Practise of Andrew

[Andrew, a busy business student, sits at his drum kit for his daily practice. Instead of fumbling with his usual pen, paper, and separate metronome, he launches your new skill-tracking application.]

[The system immediately presents him with a structured practice plan, eliminating the time he usually wastes deciding what to play. He selects a rudiment exercise. As he plays, he utilizes the app's integrated tools, keeping his focus entirely on his stick control.]
